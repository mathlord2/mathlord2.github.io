Projects and Awards

Hacks/Projects
Jamhacks 2 (2018) - Kinetic Alpha

Jamhacks 2 was my first hackathon that I have ever attended, which was hosted by my own high school. Our team of 4 created a new tab extension which provides a variety of uses, including links to educational services, the date and time, and a Google search bar. The changing backgrounds always supply a fresh look for the new tab.



Starterhacks (2019) - Byte-Mail

At Starterhacks 2019, hosted by the University of Waterloo, our team of 5 created a secure mailing platform. It allows the sender's email to be encrypted using a blockchain-inspired algorithm and sent anonymously to the receiver, without anyone being able to hack into the message and figure out its sender or recipient.



MasseyHacks V (2019) - Spent

At MasseyHacks V in Windsor, out team of 4 created a web application called Spent. This Firebase app allows you to track your expenses over a specific time period and visualize how much you went over/below your budget.



JAMHacks 3 - OfflineAssist

At JAMHacks 3, our team of 4 created OfflineAssist, an offline Google-services provider, with the main service being Google Assistant. The user sends an SMS to a Twilio number, which sends and receives requests from the Google APIs. Our team earned 2nd place.



Stay tuned for more projects!
